About Decoy Our Story

幫妳雕回 Rediscover 原本最美的線條。 Your most beautiful self.

每個人的身體都有它本來的樣子——那個線條，那個弧度，那個妳在某個早晨照鏡子時，突然認出的自己。

Every body has its own original form — those lines, those curves, that version of yourself you catch in the mirror one morning and suddenly recognise.

科技不是讓妳成為別人的樣子，而是幫妳找回妳本來就有、卻還沒完全活出來的狀態。

Technology isn't here to make you someone else. It's here to help you return to who you already are.

我們知道有多少女孩，因為對身形的不滿意，而悄悄失去了一些自信。不是因為她們不夠美，而是沒有人告訴她們：妳本來就有屬於自己的獨特美麗。

We know how many women quietly lose confidence because of how they feel about their bodies — not because they aren't beautiful, but because no one told them: you already have a beauty that is entirely your own.

Decoy 創立的初衷，是想讓每一個人都能用安全、溫柔、有效的方式，重新認識自己的身體——不是靠破壞，不是靠痛苦，而是靠科技，靠尊重，靠相信妳本來就值得。

Decoy was founded with one purpose: to help everyone reconnect with their body through safe, gentle, effective means — not through harm, not through pain, but through technology, respect, and the belief that you deserve this.
