Self Assessment
您的場域準備好了嗎？ Is your venue ready?
在聯繫我們之前，建議先思考以下問題。越多答案是「是」，合作成功率越高。 Before reaching out, consider the following. The more questions you answer "yes" to, the stronger the foundation for a successful partnership.
Q1
您的場域目前已有穩定的客群，還是正在尋找吸引新客的差異化服務？ Does your venue already have a stable clientele, or are you looking for a differentiating service to attract new clients?
兩種狀況都適合，但導入策略不同。有穩定客群者可直接轉換，正在拓客者可作為主打賣點。 Both work — but the strategy differs. Stable clientele = direct conversion opportunity. Growing venue = a strong acquisition differentiator.
Q2
您的目標客群是否為有消費能力、重視品質與科學背書的族群？ Is your target clientele quality-conscious, with spending power and an appreciation for science-backed services?
Decoy 的定位是高端科技保養，適合願意為真實效果付出對應費用的客群，不適合以低價競爭為主的市場。 Decoy is positioned as premium technology-based care — suited for clients willing to invest in genuine results, not price-competitive markets.
Q3
您的團隊是否有意願接受培訓，並能持續提供專業的客戶諮詢服務？ Is your team willing to undergo training and consistently deliver professional client consultations?
Decoy 的價值來自專業說明與數據記錄。操作員的諮詢能力，是客戶回購率的關鍵。 Decoy's value comes from professional consultation and data tracking. Operator quality is the key driver of rebooking.
Q4
您的場域是否有獨立的保養服務空間，能夠提供私密、舒適的保養體驗？ Does your venue have a dedicated treatment space offering a private, comfortable care experience?
Decoy 的保養體驗設計上強調私密性與舒適感，建議有相對獨立的服務空間。 The Decoy experience is designed around privacy and comfort. A relatively private service space is recommended.
如果您對以上問題有疑問，或想了解更多導入細節，歡迎直接聯繫我們——沒有標準答案，只有最適合您場域的方案。 If you have questions about the above, or want to understand the implementation in more detail, reach out directly — there are no standard answers, only the right solution for your venue.
