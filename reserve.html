FAQ
妳可能想問的 Questions you might have
第一次需要做什麼準備嗎？ Do I need to prepare anything for my first visit? +
不需要特別準備。穿著舒適的衣服來就好，我們會在現場做說明和測量。 No special preparation needed. Just wear comfortable clothing — we'll take care of the rest on arrival.
保養過程會痛嗎？ Will the session be painful? +
Decoy 採非侵入式設計，多數使用者表示過程輕柔、無明顯不適感。個別感受因人而異。 Decoy uses a non-invasive design. Most users report a gentle experience with no significant discomfort. Individual experiences may vary.
保養之後需要休息或避免什麼嗎？ Are there any restrictions after a session? +
保養結束後可以直接正常生活、運動、工作，沒有特別的限制。 You can return to normal activities immediately after your session — exercise, work, social plans. No special restrictions.
一次保養大概多久？ How long does a session take? +
初次到訪含諮詢和測量約 20–30 分鐘。後續保養課程約 30–40 分鐘，依部位而定。 Your first visit including consultation and measurement takes around 20–30 minutes. Subsequent sessions are approximately 30–40 minutes depending on the area.
在哪裡可以體驗 Decoy？ Where can I experience Decoy? +
目前 Decoy 在台北設有服務據點。請透過 LINE 或 Email 聯繫我們，我們會告訴妳最近的服務地點。 Decoy currently has service locations in Taipei. Please contact us via LINE or email and we'll let you know the nearest location.
費用大概是多少？ How much does it cost? +
費用依保養部位和課程方案而定。請聯繫我們，我們會根據妳的需求提供詳細說明。 Pricing depends on the area and programme. Please contact us and we'll provide details based on your needs.
準備好了嗎？ Ready to begin?
帶著妳自己來，我們從好好認識妳開始。 Just come as you are. We start by getting to know you.
