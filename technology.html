Decoy 迅So槍的核心是一套三層穿透系統。精華液成分從表皮到細胞，每一層都有它的作用路徑。本頁說明的是成分的生化機制，以及傳導器如何協助成分抵達作用位置。 The Decoy device operates through a three-layer delivery system. The active ingredients in the serum work through distinct biochemical pathways at each layer. This page explains how the formulation works and how the device assists ingredient delivery to the target site.

How It Works
三層科學穿透系統Three-Layer Science System
每一層都有它的科學依據，每一層都在為下一層做準備。Each layer is scientifically grounded. Each prepares the way for the next.
Layer 01
Macro Penetration · 宏觀穿透
超音速霧化穿透Supersonic Atomisation
氣流達到 2.5 倍音速，將精華液霧化為微奈米粒子。高速氣流產生的壓力，使微粒穿越角質層的物理屏障，抵達真皮層深度。角質層屏障是外用成分滲透的主要限制，此機制透過物理壓力而非化學促進劑突破該屏障。 Airflow accelerates to 2.5× the speed of sound, atomising the serum into micro-nanometre particles. The resulting pressure allows particles to cross the stratum corneum — the primary barrier to ingredient penetration — reaching the deep dermis without chemical penetration enhancers.

Layer 02
CPP Delivery · 細胞穿透遞送
細胞穿透勝肽遞送Cell-Penetrating Peptide Delivery
精華液配方含 sh-Decapeptide-5 SP，屬細胞穿透勝肽（CPP）類成分。CPP 的生化特性使其能攜帶活性成分穿越磷脂雙分子層，進入細胞內部。體外實驗數據顯示：此成分於 15 分鐘內進入角質細胞，2 小時完成組織層滲透，成分到達率較一般外用配方提升 24 倍。 The formulation contains sh-Decapeptide-5 SP, a cell-penetrating peptide (CPP). The biochemical properties of CPPs allow them to carry active ingredients across the phospholipid bilayer into the cell interior. In vitro data shows entry into keratinocytes within 15 minutes, full tissue penetration within 2 hours, with a 24× improvement in delivery rate versus standard topical application.

Layer 03
cAMP Signal · 代謝訊號啟動
脂解代謝訊號啟動Metabolic Signal Activation
配方中的活性成分，在學術研究層面已被記錄與 cAMP 訊號路徑相關：cAMP 上升 → PKA 活化 → HSL 與 ATGL 磷酸化 → 三酸甘油酯水解為甘油與游離脂肪酸。此為成分的體外研究機制描述；Decoy 精華液配方以此為科學設計基礎，搭配 CPP 遞送系統與設備穿透，使成分得以抵達作用位置。個別使用者的實際感受與效果因人而異。 Active ingredients in the formulation (including phosphodiesterase inhibitors such as caffeine) inhibit PDE upon entering the cell, elevating intracellular cAMP. cAMP, as a second messenger, activates PKA, which phosphorylates HSL and ATGL, initiating triglyceride hydrolysis to glycerol and free fatty acids. This describes the established biochemical action of the formulation's ingredients. Fat cells remain structurally intact throughout — no cytotoxic mechanism is involved.

非侵入式傳導設備不產生傷口、不引起組織反應期，因此無需恢復期限制。施作後可立即正常活動。Non-invasive delivery devices do not create wounds or induce a tissue recovery response. Normal activity can resume immediately after each session.

這不是你喝的那杯咖啡。化妝品級純化咖啡因在體外研究中顯示，能夠抑制磷酸二酯酶（PDE）的活性——PDE 是負責分解 cAMP 的酵素。cAMP 在細胞生化研究中被記錄為與脂肪代謝路徑相關的訊號分子。此為成分的體外研究特性描述，非對人體使用效果之宣稱。EWG 安全評等 1（最高等級）。 Not the coffee you drink.
